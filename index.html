SHOPEE BRAND WEBSITE

DESIGN DEFINITION

Grid System

Our website content layout follows a grid system with a maximum width of 1260px. If there are cards and images, they are arranged and sized as shown in the figure below. Exceptions: 1. If there are more than four elements in a row, then you can decide their layout individually. 2. Some buttons will be wider than the maximum width of the content. 3. banners are not limited by the maximum width.

Big Banner Response

Big Banner Response

We wanted the banner to be huge and to define the atmosphere of the site. the banner is actually an outer frame with the image inside. the banner is always 100% browser width with a fixed height. As for the image, it always fits on the largest border and cannot be squeezed out of shape.

width: 100%;
min-width: 1440px;
